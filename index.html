What is Spotify?

Spotify is a platform for well known and lesser known artists to put out their music to the world. Spotify along with a few others, is the most popular app to stream music and produce music. Spotify is available on a lot of platforms, some of those include

  • Phones and Ipads
  • Laptops and desktops
  • Video game consols, etc
